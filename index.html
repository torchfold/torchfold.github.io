TorchFold: A PyTorch & NPU
Compatible AlphaFold3

Democratizing biomolecular modeling with a native PyTorch implementation.

TorchFold overcomes the framework dependencies and restrictive licensing of AlphaFold3. We provide a complete PyTorch reimplementation optimized for both NVIDIA GPUs and Huawei Ascend NPUs, achieving state-of-the-art performance with commercially friendly open weights.

Key Contributions

Native PyTorch

A complete migration from JAX/Haiku to native nn.Module. Enables seamless integration with the broader deep learning ecosystem.

NPU Optimization

Optimized for Huawei Ascend NPUs via CANN backend, featuring fused attention operators for high-performance inference.

Open License

We will release commercially friendly model weights under the MIT license, removing barriers for industry research and deployment.

Numerical Alignment with AF3

Validating faithful reproduction of the original AF3 implementation across protein monomers and antibody-antigen complexes.
